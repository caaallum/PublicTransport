Our Vision

Sustainable

We aim to provide eco friendly, sustainable methods of transport. We will do this through a fully electrified fleet using both Li-Ion batteries and Hydrogen Fuel Cells. This will lead to cleaner, higher quality air in our urban areas and help preserve green spaces and wildlife.

Efficient

Using our eco friendly fleet also comes with the added benefits of higher efficieny, meaning more miles out of your chosen fuel source, driving costs and emissions down even further.

Responsive

With advanced algorithms which look at real time customer demand and climatic data we can dispatch the perfect number of services resulting in less wasted miles during off-peak times and more options to reduce delays and crowding at rush hour.

Reliability

Using automated driving alongside our responsive approach to load balancing we can reduce the amount of disruption to services. This will mean a much more reliable timetable, resulting in restored trust in the public transport sector and increasing passenger confidence.

Cost Effective

Of course everything comes at a price, but the aim of our system is to eliminate high upkeep costs. With our system in place money can be saved across fuel, maintenance and driver salaries. This saving will offset the higher capital expenditure from investing in the technology and will eventually pay for itself.

Simplifed

Making it easier and simpler to use multiple forms of public transport through combined ticketing and tap on/tap off systems will make it a lot easier and more accesible for customers to use leading to an increase in passenger numbers, particularly across urban areas.
